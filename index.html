Our Beutiful Home

Located just 12 km from Meteora, Mary's country house near meteora features accommodation in Kalabaka with access to a garden, a terrace, as well as a concierge service. This holiday home provides free private parking, a 24-hour front desk and free WiFi. The air-conditioned holiday home consists of 1 bedroom, a living room, a fully equipped kitchen with a fridge and a coffee machine, and 1 bathroom with a bath and a hairdryer. Towels and bed linen are provided in the holiday home. A children's playground is available for guests to use at the holiday home. Agios Nikolaos Anapafsas is 12 km from Mary's country house near meteora, while Monastery of Agios Stefanos is 13 km away. The nearest airport is Ioannina, 109 km from the accommodation, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
